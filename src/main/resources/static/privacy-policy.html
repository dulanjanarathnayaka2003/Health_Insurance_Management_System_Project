Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how HealthInsure collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly, including but not limited to:

  • Identity Data: Name, date of birth, national identification numbers
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, postal address
  • Financial Data: Bank account details, payment card information
  • Medical Data: Health information, medical history, treatment records
  • Policy Data: Insurance policy details, coverage information, claims history

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our services, we automatically collect:

  • Device information and IP address
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Usage data and interaction patterns
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Policy Administration: To underwrite, issue, and manage your insurance policies
  • Claims Processing: To evaluate and process insurance claims
  • Customer Service: To provide support and respond to inquiries
  • Marketing: To send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Legal Compliance: To meet regulatory and legal requirements
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent activities

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

  • Contract: To fulfill our obligations under insurance policies
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with insurance and financial regulations
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and prevent fraud
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional services

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Insurance Partners: Reinsurers and underwriting partners
  • Service Providers: IT vendors, payment processors, and administrative services
  • Healthcare Providers: Hospitals and medical professionals for claims processing
  • Regulatory Authorities: Government agencies as required by law
  • Legal Entities: Courts, law enforcement, and legal advisors when necessary

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Staff training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Administer your insurance policies
  • Process claims and provide customer service
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Defend against legal claims

After the retention period, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction: Limit the processing of your data
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another provider
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent at any time

8. International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Sri Lanka. When we do so, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain session information and user preferences
  • Analyze website usage and improve our services
  • Provide personalized content and advertising

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through appropriate channels. The effective date will be updated accordingly.
